The Best Movies I ever watched

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shiddat

Love-struck Jaggi can cross the seven seas for his dream girl, Kartika. Even if it means stopping her wedding

12th FAil

IPS officer Manoj Kumar Sharma fearlessly embraces the idea of restarting his academic journey and reclaiming his destiny at a place where millions of students attempt the world's toughest competitive exam: UPSC.

Dada

A college student embraces fatherhood and provides unconditional care and love to his child while facing the challenges of life as a single parent.
